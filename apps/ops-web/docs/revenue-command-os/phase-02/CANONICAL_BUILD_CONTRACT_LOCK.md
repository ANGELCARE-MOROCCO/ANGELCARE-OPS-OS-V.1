## Immutable mandate

Mega ZIP 2 creates the machine-readable commercial truth layer required by the future Revenue Command OS. It models how AngelCare creates, prices, sells, delivers, protects, renews and expands revenue without activating autonomous strategy execution.

The accepted scope includes:

- AngelCare business units and their revenue models;
- governed offers, formats, commercial promises and availability;
- bundles, prerequisites and offer relationships;
- customer segments and buying logic;
- decision-maker authority, motivations and evidence expectations;
- Moroccan markets, cities, territories and delivery readiness;
- sales channels and stage-based commercial journeys;
- pricing models, protected thresholds, costs, margins and discount authority;
- commercial, training, caregiver, operational and inventory capacities;
- delivery constraints and revenue dependency gates;
- seasonal windows and preparation lead times;
- cross-sell, upsell, renewal and referral paths;
- completeness scoring, contradiction detection and model validation;
- governed backend mutations, model versions and immutable audit.

## Non-negotiable safety posture

- Execution mode remains `shadow`.
- External actions remain disabled.
- Strategy execution remains disabled.
- Unknown prices, margins, stock and capacity are never invented.
- Missing data is represented as `needs-validation` and may block future recommendations.
- Database access is server-side only through controlled repository functions.
- Client-side direct table access remains revoked.
- Every mutation and validation cycle is auditable.
- Existing `/revenue-command-center` remains untouched and operational.

## Phase boundary

Mega ZIP 2 does not claim to provide the 3,000-command library, live signal fabric, Strategy Brain, Validation Council, mission compilation or autonomous outreach. It provides the commercial truth those future phases must query and obey.
