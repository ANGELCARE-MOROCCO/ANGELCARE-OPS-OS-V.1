# Mega ZIP 2 Acceptance Contract

Mega ZIP 2 is accepted only when all conditions below pass.

## Functional

- Revenue Digital Twin route is protected and navigable.
- Fourteen purpose-built sections are available.
- Business units, offers, bundles, segments, decision makers, markets, channels, journeys, price rules, capacities, dependencies, seasonal windows and growth paths are persisted or explicitly served as contract seeds.
- Governed create/update/retire operations work through the backend API.
- Validation issues can be acknowledged, resolved or waived.
- Model completeness can be recalculated and versioned.

## Business truth

- At least eight AngelCare business units are represented.
- Academy, Home Service, Flashcards, Kindergarten, Hospitality and Corporate offers are represented.
- Casablanca, Rabat, Kénitra, Tanger, Marrakech, Fès and Agadir are modeled.
- Known caregiver capacity assumptions are represented with provenance.
- Unknown prices, margins, stock and standards remain unresolved rather than invented.

## Security

- Shadow mode remains active.
- External action and strategy execution flags remain disabled.
- Client access to Phase 2 tables is revoked.
- RLS is enabled.
- API entity, field and action allow-lists are enforced.
- Mutations and validations are audited.

## Regression

- Phase 1 static acceptance remains green.
- Phase 2 static acceptance remains green.
- Targeted TypeScript validation passes.
- Legacy Revenue Command Center remains unchanged.
