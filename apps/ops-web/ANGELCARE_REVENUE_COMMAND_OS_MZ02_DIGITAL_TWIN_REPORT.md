# Ops Web Integration — Revenue Digital Twin

The Phase 2 integration is isolated under `/revenue-command-os/digital-twin` and extends the existing Revenue Command OS foundation without replacing `/revenue-command-center`.

## Main application surfaces

- Executive Digital Twin overview
- Business unit portfolio
- Offer dossiers
- Bundles and offer relationships
- Customer segment intelligence
- Decision-maker intelligence
- Market and territory readiness
- Channel and sales journey architecture
- Price and margin protection
- Capacity and delivery feasibility
- Seasonality
- Expansion and renewal graph
- Revenue dependencies
- Model validation and governed corrections

See `docs/revenue-command-os/phase-02` for installation, API, data dictionary, security, rollback and acceptance evidence.
