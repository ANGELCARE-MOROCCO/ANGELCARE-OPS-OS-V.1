# Acceptance MZ12

MZ12 is accepted when:

- the fourteen canonical workspaces are present, with sixteen implemented operational zones;
- all twelve executive actions are available;
- MZ10 context and MZ11 reviews remain traceable;
- facts, assumptions, contradictions, evidence and Red-Team findings are visually distinct;
- capacity, constraint and outcome simulations are labeled as simulations;
- approval targets an immutable strategy version;
- approval classes, roles and multi-decision requirements are enforced;
- conditional approvals are machine-readable;
- amendments and combinations create new lineage and require revalidation;
- executive decision memos are versioned;
- only approved, unblocked, condition-satisfied versions become ready for MZ13;
- RLS, service-only persistence, audit, idempotency and rollback pass;
- external actions remain exactly zero.
